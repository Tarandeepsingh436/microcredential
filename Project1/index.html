New York, often called New York City (NYC) to distinguish it from the State of New York, is the most populous city in the United States. With a 2020 population of 8,804,190 distributed over 300.46 square miles (778.2 km2), New York City is also the most densely populated major city in the United States. Located at the southern tip of the state of New York, the city is the center of the New York metropolitan area, the largest metropolitan area in the world by urban area.

New York City Office Leasing Surpassed 20 Million Square Feet in 2021

CBRE reports leasing activity in the Manhattan office market totaled 8.19 million sq. ft. in Q4, 30% ahead of the five-year quarterly average of 6.27 million sq. ft. Full-year leasing activity of 20.42 million sq. ft. was up 63% compared with the same period last year but lagged the five-year average by 19%.



Goodbye to cash tolls, and some notorious history, at bridge

Starting Sunday, drivers looking to cross the Hudson River from New Jersey into New York will go through an electronic tolling system. Drivers without E-ZPass who would otherwise be paying cash will instead have their license plates photographed by overhead cameras and bills sent to them by mail.
